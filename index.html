About Miss DeWinne


Hi! My name is Morgan DeWinne and I am currently a senior at Monmouth University and working towards my Bachelor's degree in English and Secondary Education with a certification in Special Education. I have always had a passion for teaching, and I constantly look forward to sharing this passion with my students in the subject of English. I am from Vineland, New Jersey, but currently live in Ocean, New Jersey. My goals as a teacher are to give each of my students the best learning environment and experience possible, which I plan on achieving through differentiation and communication. My teaching adventures have only just begun, but I look forward to furthering my career and doing what I love the most: teaching!
